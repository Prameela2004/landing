COURSES WE OFFER

We offer a diverse range of cutting-edge courses in areas such as Pre University Course,Bachelors of Technology

PUC-MPC

The specific curriculum and focus areas may vary slightly between different IIIT campuses, but generally, MPC programs aim to produce graduates with a strong foundation in both mathematics and computing, enabling them to tackle complex problems in various industries and research domains.

PUC-MBIPC

IITs have recently introduced an MBIPC program, it would signify a departure from their traditional focus on engineering and technology-related disciplines. Introducing a program in Mathematics, Biology, Physics,chemistry, potentially catering to students interested in interdisciplinary fields.

BTECH

B.Tech programs in IIITs offer specialized education in fields like Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and Information Technology. These programs equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge for careers in software development.

IIIT HUBS

The four IIIT campuses in Andhra Pradesh, India, namely IIIT Nuzvid, IIIT Srikakulam, IIIT RK Valley, and IIIT Ongole, are renowned for their rigorous academic programs in engineering and technology.
